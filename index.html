About Me

My name is Andrew Stehno. I am currently a student at the University of Utah, enrolled in the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. The bootcamp is a high intensity, fast paced six month program covering front end and back end development. At the conclusion of this course, I look forward to utilizing my new skills, as well as some old ones, to be a part of and contribute to Utah's booming tech industry.

My wife and I currently live in South Jordan and keep busy with the vast amount of recreation available to us. We enjoy hiking, sailing, offroading in our Jeep, and working with our dogs.
